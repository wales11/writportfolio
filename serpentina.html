Serpentina Arts Project

My Serpentina Arts project was conducted via email correspondence, Zoom interviews, and in-person meetings. I collaborated with a partner from my class and an artist from Serpentina Arts to make an artist bio for them that they can use in professional and personal pursuits. I gained experience in a new area of writing, as well as gained exposure into the art world.

It was my first attempt at making a static site. I used Markdown and MkDocs to publish my first static site on Github pages, and I had a lot of fun doing it. I've been interested in making websites and have used Content Management Systems previously but always wanted to make one myself. I am thankful that this class was able to let me gain this experience.
