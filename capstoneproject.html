Senior Capstone Project

My senior capstone project tackles the issue of providing education for the average baseball fan on the topic of advanced statistics, often called sabermetrics. For my senior capstone project, I conducted both primary research and secondary research. My primary research was done in the form of interviews with casual baseball fans and industry professionals working in MLB front offices. I received varied feedback between the two groups, and I took that feedback into account when coming to a conclusion and making recommendations at the end of my capstone project.

I was excited to take on this capstone proejct because it combines my immense love and passion for baseball with my career as a technical writer. I gained a lot of experience about the baseball world and how other people think about sabermetrics, the future of baseball, and the future of broadcasting. I incorporated all of these things in my capstone project, as well as a plan for the future. This capstone project is something I will be working on going forward, as it is a project very close to my personal and professional goals.
